Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the Sustainable Business Leaders Network Retreat?

The Sustainable Business Leaders Network Retreat is an annual event that brings together professionals, alumni, and current students from sustainability fields to connect, learn, and collaborate. The retreat focuses on fostering strong, lasting relationships, sharing knowledge, and exploring the latest trends and challenges in sustainable business practices.

2. Who is the retreat for?

The retreat is primarily for Bard MBA alumni, current students, and professionals working in sustainability-related fields. It’s designed to provide a space for those who are passionate about sustainability to reenergize their efforts, expand their networks, and gain actionable insights for their careers.

3. Where and when is the retreat being held?

The retreat will be held Sep 19 & 20th at TBD venue in NYC, preceding NYC Climate Week. Check our Schedule page for a detailed breakdown of the event agenda.

5. How do I register?

You can register through our Eventbrite page here. If you’re facing financial hardship or the registration fee is preventing you from attending, please reach out to us via the contact form for assistance.

6. Are meals included?

Yes, meals are included for both days of the retreat. Breakfast, lunch, and coffee breaks will be provided, and we will have an optional social mixer on the first evening. For more details, please refer to the Schedule page.

7. What is the format of the event?

The retreat includes a mix of facilitated discussions, breakout sessions, networking activities, and awards. There will also be time for informal coffee chats, exhibit sessions, and a social mixer to help foster deeper connections.

8. What should I bring to the retreat?

We recommend bringing a notebook, virtual business cards, and any relevant materials if you'd like help brainstorming on a specific problem. Dress is business casual.

9. Can I attend virtually?

At this time, the retreat is an in-person event to maximize networking opportunities and hands-on learning experiences. However, we are exploring the possibility of offering virtual options in the future.

10. How do I become a member?

If you're interested in becoming a member or learning more about membership benefits, please contact us or visit our Membership page for more details.
