Our Network

Our network is a vibrant community of sustainability professionals dedicated to fostering connections, sharing knowledge, and supporting each other in our endeavors. We invite you to join our LinkedIn page where you can stay updated on announcements, events, and engage with fellow members.

You can access our LinkedIn group here. This group serves as a bulletin board for members to shout out their accomplishments, ask questions, and foster collaboration within our community.

Please note that membership in our LinkedIn group requires you to fill out the membership form, which includes an option for free membership. Additionally, when completing the form, we encourage you to indicate if you are willing to connect as a mentor or peer for those seeking guidance on specific questions or inquiries.
